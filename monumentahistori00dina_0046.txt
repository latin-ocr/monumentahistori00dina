The first extract given below was written at Athens
about 444 Rc. In it Herodotus makes the Danube
rise near the city Pyrene. This gross error is not
repeated in the second extract, which was written some
years later at Thurii. Here we are told the Danube
rises in the country of the Celts, who after the Cynetes
are the last people in Western Europe. The Cynetes
dwelt in the extreme south of PortugaLl and the Oelts
consequently in circa B.c. 442 extended over the south
